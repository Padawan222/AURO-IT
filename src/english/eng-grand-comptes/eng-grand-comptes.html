We offer to large enterprises our expertise in project management and data management.

We deliver the same level of service quality as the BIG 4 in terms of tools and methodologies.

What sets us apart from the BIG 4 :

Tailored service versus one-size-fits-all solutions :

Big consulting firms sometimes provide "one-size-fits-all" project management solutions that do not consider the unique needs and circumstances of each organization.
This can result in solutions that are not fully aligned with the company's goals and objectives, leading to inefficiencies.

Every organization has a project culture.
We quickly grasp this culture to propose a unique approach that will be efficient and ensure the project's success.

Agility versus lack of flexibility :

Large consulting firms may have rigid processes and methodologies that can limit their ability to adapt to changing circumstances or unexpected events.
This can make it challenging to effectively manage projects in dynamic or uncertain environments.

Our mantra is "less is more."
We know how to achieve more with less and adapt to the context, changes, and specific constraints.
We do not apply a one-size-fits-all approach.
Our expertise, experience, and collaborative thinking enable us to collectively reach your objectives.

Commitment versus limited involvement :

In some cases, the Big 4 may not fully engage in the project management process and delegate a significant portion of the work to junior staff or subcontractors.
can result in a lack of consistency and quality control, impacting the overall success of the project.

We guarantee consistency among all internal and external teams working on the project.
We are also here to act, not just play the role of a controller.
We work collaboratively with your teams to ensure overall alignment in terms of methodologies and communication.
We are capable of coaching junior teams as well.
