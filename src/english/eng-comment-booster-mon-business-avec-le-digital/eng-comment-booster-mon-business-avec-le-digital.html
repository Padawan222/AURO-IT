There is no magic formula to boost your business with digital.
Just having a website or planning to launch a mobile app won't necessarily increase your sales.
Digital offers a plethora of solutions from the simplest to the most sophisticated.
The real question is to determine which 20% of these solutions will bring you 80% of the benefits.
And this is the challenge we enjoy taking on.

AURO IT offers you customized solutions that respect your original culture.
It's not about adapting to digital it's about digital adapting to you.
What does it mean to adapt digital to your business in practical terms ?
After supporting hundreds of SMEs in Europe, we realized that we consistently work on two areas :

productivity and turnover, resulting in an average growth of 10%.

PRODUCTIVITY

In terms of productivity, there are several examples of time savings and therefore money.
We never apply a one-size-fits-all approach we start from your reality and together we build the offer that will adapt to your history, culture, strategy, and market.
For this, we have developed a methodology that allows us to get to know you without wasting your time with unnecessary meetings.
We call this TOTAL IMMERSION.

The productivity examples below are indicative and not exhaustive.

Customer Service :

Use of chatbots to handle common customer questions and problems.
Automated ticketing systems that direct customer support requests to the appropriate department or personnel.

Marketing :

Email marketing campaigns triggered by customer behavior, such as abandoned shopping carts or recent purchases.
Automatic scheduling of social media posts and management of advertising campaigns through automated platforms.

Sales :

Scoring and assigning leads in CRM systems, automatically prioritizing and distributing leads to sales representatives.
Automated follow-up emails or reminders to customers and prospects at various stages of the sales funnel.

Human Resources :

Employee onboarding processes, including scheduling training sessions and tracking progress.
Payroll automation to calculate hours, distribute paychecks, and manage tax withholdings.

Finance and Accounting :

Automated generation and processing of invoices, including follow-ups for late payments.
Expense management and approval workflows that simplify employee reimbursement.

Operations :

Inventory management systems that automatically reorder stock based on predefined thresholds.

Quality control processes using sensors and data analytics to detect and address manufacturing variances.

IT Management :

Automated backups and patch management to ensure the integrity and security of IT systems.
Network monitoring tools that alert IT staff to potential issues or outages.

Project Management :

Automated progress tracking and reporting, allowing real-time updates on project milestones.
Resource allocation tools that assign tasks based on availability and skill sets.

Logistics :

Route planning software for delivery fleets that optimize time and energy efficiency.
Automated tracking systems that provide customers with real-time updates on their orders.

Procurement : E-procurement systems

YOUR REVENUE GROWTH

There are several levers to increase a company's revenue.
For each of these levers, there are several digital solutions.

Product/Service Offering Optimization :

Diversification :
Add new or complementary products or services.

Quality Improvement: :
Invest in quality to justify higher prices or attract more customers.

Market Expansion :

New Markets :
Identify and target new market segments or geographic areas.

Exporting :
Consider selling your products or services internationally.

Increasing Sales to Existing Customers :

Cross-selling :
Offer complementary products to those already purchased by your clients.

Upselling :
Encourage customers to buy more expensive or superior versions of what they already buy.

Marketing and Advertising :

Digital Marketing :
Strengthen your online presence with SEO, content marketing, social media marketing, and paid advertising.

Traditional marketing:
Use traditional channels (television, radio, press) if this is relevant to your target.

Sales Force Improvement :

Sales Training :
Improve your sales team's skills.

Commission System :
Implement or optimize a variable compensation system to motivate your salespeople.

Partnerships and Strategic Alliances :

Collaborations :
Partner with other companies to offer combined products or services.

Affiliation :

Create an affiliate program where others promote your products for a commission.

Pricing Optimization :

Pricing Strategy :
Adjust your prices based on the market and the perceived value by customers.

Promotions :
Use temporary offers to stimulate sales.

Customer Experience Improvement :

Customer Service :
Invest in quality customer service to encourage loyalty.

Customer Journey :
Simplify the buying process to reduce friction.

Technology and Innovation :

Automation :
Automate processes to reduce costs and increase efficiency.

E-commerce :
Develop or optimize your online store.

Data Analysis :

Data-driven :
Make decisions based on the analysis of sales and market data.

Reputation Management :

Online Reviews :
Encourage positive online reviews to attract new customers.

Our mission at AURO IT is to help you identify the best lever for your business and to offer you the most suitable digital solution.
