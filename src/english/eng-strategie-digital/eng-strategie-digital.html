Our network of experts serving your digital strategy always consists of a team of two :

 an IT expert and a business/industry expert. This is done to offer you the most suitable strategy.

This team starts by conducting an audit of your products, your customers, and your strategy to better understand your objectives.
Then, it identifies the opportunities that digital solutions could bring you.
Our goal is to do more with less :
to provide you with the 20% of digital solutions that will allow you to achieve an 80% benefit.

Therefore, we help you choose from a variety of services or tools and show you concretely why they are the most appropriate for you.
Your digital strategy is a comprehensive plan that describes your approach to harnessing digital technologies, platforms, and channels to achieve your goals.
Here are the main services that are part of a digital strategy :

Extraction and processing of your data to cross-reference them with other data from your market and geographic area in order to gain a new understanding of your market, understand where you stand, and identify new opportunities.

Website development :

A digital strategy includes the design and development of a user-friendly, visually appealing website optimized for search engines.

Social media marketing :

This service involves developing a social media strategy to reach potential customers and strengthen the brand on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) :

This service involves optimizing a website to achieve a better ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs), which generates more traffic to the website.

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising :

 This service involves launching targeted advertising campaigns on search engines like Google and on social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram to generate traffic to the website.

Email marketing :

This service involves building a list of targeted email addresses and sending specific messages to subscribers to promote products or services.

Content marketing :

This service involves creating valuable and informative content such as blogs, videos, and infographics to attract potential customers and enhance brand awareness.

Mobile app development :

A digital strategy may include the development of a mobile app to improve customer interaction and enhance their overall experience.

E-commerce solutions :

This service involves developing an online store to sell products and services directly to customers.

Analytics and reporting :

A digital strategy should include tracking and analyzing metrics to measure the success of digital marketing efforts and make adjustments to the strategy if necessary.

These are some of the main services of a digital strategy, but there may be other services depending on the organization's objectives.
